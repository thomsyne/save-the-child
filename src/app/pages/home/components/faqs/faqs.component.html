FAQs

Onboarding

When I fill the sign-up form to join RexRetail, what is next?

Your details will be used to onboard you onto the platform. Thereafter, you will receive an email giving you details on how to complete your registration on the platform.

How soon will I get my POS delivered?

This will happen within 2-10 working days of receiving your accurate business address details on the platform.

How do I add or remove inventory in my store?

Select My Shops, open selected shop, select Inventory, you will see a display of all products available in that store. Go on to add or remove inventory.

How do I see a list of my transactions?

Select My Reports, open Sales Reports and you can see the details of the sales consummated in your shop, and the cashier who facilitated the sale.

What happens if I have an issue with a payment or any complaint?

Select Complaints, enter details of the issue and you will receive a response within 1-4 days. Alternatively, you can call the support line, 0700-ACCELEREX.

Payments

How can I collect payments from my customers?

You can collect payments in cash and card.

How can I pay for my subscription?

There are two forms:

  1. Through your settlement. This means the money will be deducted from your wallet when the subscription payment is due.
  2. Card payment on the POS
    • Login to your RexRetail account on your POS Device.
    • Navigate to the Subscription Page.
    • Select desired subscription plan.
    • Insert your debit card.
    • Enter your debit card pin.
    • Click on the "Pay Now" button.

What card types can I accept on the POS?

Both credit and debit cards are accepted on the POS. These include: Mastercard, Visa and Verve cards.
